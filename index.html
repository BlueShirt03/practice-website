How to Prepare for an Interview

It is important to prepare for a job interview. First, make sure that you have completely read the job description. Write down any questions about the position so that you can ask them during the interview. Thoroughly research the company. Know when the business started, the name of the CEO or company president, and what products and services are offered.

Know what you are going to wear to the interview. Dress your best. Make sure that you clothes are not wrinkled. Make sure that you are well groomed and look presentable. First impression are important. Greet everyone you meet with a simle, beginning with the first person you see once you enter the building.

  • Research the company.
  • Practice answering interview questions.
  • Dress for success.
  • Bring a copy of your resume and a portfolio of your work.
  • Follow up with a Thank You letter.
